Multikernel Advantages

While virtual machines add a heavy hypervisor layer and containers share a single kernel, our multikernel approach creates independent, specialized kernels that directly access hardware resources with minimal overhead.

Our approach combines the security of VMs with the performance of containers and the flexibility of custom kernels.
