About Multikernel Technologies

We build split-kernel architecture for Linux, separating application logic from device processing into independent kernels for better performance, isolation, and reliability.

Our Technology Contact Us

Our Mission

To give every application predictable performance by splitting the Linux kernel into two: an app-kernel that runs your code with zero interrupt interference, and a device-kernel that handles all I/O and drivers. Built entirely on upstream Linux, 100% open source.

Our Vision

A world where applications run on dedicated kernel instances with clean separation from device drivers and interrupts. No tuning, no workarounds. Each kernel has its own lifecycle, its own failure domain, and its own update path, all while staying within the Linux ecosystem.

Cong Wang

Founder & CEO

Cong Wang is a Linux kernel developer with 16 years of experience and maintainer of the networking traffic control subsystem since 2017. He has contributed over 1000 commits to the Linux kernel. Prior to founding Multikernel Technologies, he led a software engineering team at ByteDance. His deep expertise in kernel networking and memory management led to the development of the split-kernel architecture that powers Multikernel today.
